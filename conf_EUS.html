Engineering Undergraduate Society (EUS) Conference

When is it?

This conference typically happens in September at the beginning of the school year.

What is it?

A one-day crash course on the Engineering Undergraduate Society (EUS). You'll get to attend sessions on topics such as governance, leadership, event planning, EUS resources and mental health, all while networking with other EUS groups.

Where is it?

This conference always happens right on McGill campus! Keep an eye on Facebook for details.

Why should I go?

- To find out about the EUS and how it functions

- To see if you would like to be a part of its many groups and clubs
